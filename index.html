Hello,

I am Ibrahim Kabore, a Master's student in Geomatics, Remote Sensing, GIS, and Environmental Science at FST Beni Mellal. My thesis focuses on “Harmonisation of Moroccan Soil Spectral Libraries (Vis-NIR)” at the Center for Remote Sensing Applications (CRSA), part of the Mohammed VI Polytechnic University (UM6P), Morocco.

I apply Soil Spectroscopy and Machine Learning techniques to predict, classify, and map soil properties. This contributes to the UM6P and OCP Group's vision for sustainable agriculture and eco-friendly land management. Supervised by Prof. Abderrazak EL HARTI and Prof. Mohamed Bayad.

Besides academics, I am a certified personal trainer passionate about fitness, self-discipline, and personal development.
